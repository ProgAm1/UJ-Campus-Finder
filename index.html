Helpful Advice

Campus Tips

Quick tips to increase your chances of recovering a lost item.

Tip 01

Report Immediately

The sooner you submit a report, the higher the chance someone has already found your item and filed a matching report.

Tip 02

Be Specific

Include as much detail as possible: color, brand, model number, and the exact location where you last had the item.

Tip 03

Check Regularly

New reports are added every day. Return to the Reports page often to see if someone filed a matching found report.
